JAI ODISHA SOCIAL ORGANISATION

Jai Odisha is a voluntary social organization based in Bhubaneswar, established in 2012 under the leadership of Mr.Rudra Narayan Samantaray. Dedicated to building a compassionate and inclusive society, the organization works in social welfare, community service, youth engagement, cultural exchange, and humanitarian support.

Over the years, Jai Odisha has launched impactful initiatives such as support services for international students and Sesha Saathi, a free hearse service for underprivileged families. With a vision of treating Odisha as one extended family, the organization continues to promote social responsibility, community participation, and support for those in need, contributing to a stronger and more caring Odisha.

The objective of Jai Odisha, a voluntary youth organization based in Bhubaneswar, is to facilitate the socio-economic upliftment of the people of Odisha. The forum is dedicated to empowering local communities and improving the quality of life for vulnerable populations through targeted initiatives.
