Who are we?

Committed to practical help and steady community service.

Jai Odisha is a social organisation based in Bhubaneswar, Odisha dedicated to serving underprivileged communities across Bhubaneswar. We are committed to creating a society where every individual has equal opportunities for education, healthcare, livelihood and social development.

With honesty, dedication and the support of our well-wishers, Jai Odisha Social Organisation continues its journey towards social justice and human welfare.
